My Art

I got started in art at an early age, drawing cars and shoddy perspective drawings. I eventually moved my way up to traditional media in High School where I was exposed to newer techniques and tools. However I felt that it was too limiting and labor-intensive. I eventually began messing with Adobe Illustrator which was when I fell in love with digital art. As time progressed, my art style went through several iterations before I found sketches + watercolor really fun to make. I admired the level of surrealism and fantasy you were able to capture with them. But I also enjoy making other kinds of digital art too including landscapes and occasionally some graphic design assets. You can find my link to my personal RedBubble Shop below!
